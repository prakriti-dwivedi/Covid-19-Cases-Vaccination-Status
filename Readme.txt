Objective - To build a unified one-stop platform which can provide information about COVID-19 status of India at various level(states,districts,cites) and 
also show the trend of current vaccination drive in India.

Timeline:- COVID-19 - 30th January,2020 onwards 
           Vaccination- 16th January,2021 onwards


Data Source :-
